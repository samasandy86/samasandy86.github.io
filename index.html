About Me

What's Up! My name is Sama Sandy. A little about myself; I love all things and anything creative. I first cut my teeth on the creative edges a year after college. I taught myself Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign through online tutorials.

From there I transitioned into web design learning basic HTML & CSS. Outside of web design, I also taught myself video editing. I primarily use Final Cut Pro but I also use Adobe Primere Pro and After Effects. In addition to video I also dabble a bit in music production and audio editing.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen I enjoy playing volleyball with my wife of 7 (almost 8 years) and being silly with 3 and half year old daughter. We are expecting our 2nd child in June and we are overjoyed when we found out it was a boy.

If you want to get to know me on more of a professional level, check out some of my professional social profiles. If you want to see some and handful of my professional work checkout my marketing boutique company at Yayah Creative.
